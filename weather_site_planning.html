Purpose Statement

The purpose of the site will be to provide the user with information about current and upcoming weather for a given location. This weather information will include temperature and precipitation (cloudy, sunny, rain, etc.) with a slightly higher emphasis on the temperature.

Audience to Consider

The site's audience will be a decently large variety of people, though there will likely be heavier traphic from mobile users as they aim to find the current temperature. They are most likely on the site in order to know how well they need to be dressed, as well as to get an idea of whether the weather is nice enough for their oustide activities or if they should hold off for a while more. The most likely time of use is in the morning or at night while people are preparing for the current or upcoming day.

Persona

Users come to the site to learn what the current temperature is, as well as what to expect from the weather. Mobile users will most likely find the current temperature to be most important, the next most important is the current days weather, and then the five day forecast after that. Whereas desktop users are more likely to feel the current weather and the weather forecast to be more important. Desktop users are more likely trying to plan ahead, whereas mobile users are looking for more instant information.
